Degree Programmes

Bachelor Degree

"The graduate program is a basic and full-time course."

Admission Criteria

  • Applicants must pass the Matriculation Examination.
  • Follow the rules of University Entrance Guide Book.

Programme Structures

B.C.Sc

Bachelor of Computer Science

B.C.Tech

Bachelor of Computer Technology

  • • Students have to take ten semesters including internship program.
  • • Must complete specified total credit and follow criteria of credit system (attendance, quizzes, assignments, test, and exam).
  • • Qualified students can join the Master Degree program upon completion.

Master Degree

Advanced Research & Thesis

Programme Structures

  • M.C.Sc (Master of Computer Science)
  • M.C.Tech (Master of Computer Technology)

Students have to take two-semester course work and complete a master thesis.
