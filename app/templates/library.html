About the Library

The University of Computer Studies, Taungoo library is the heart of the campus, providing a conducive environment for learning and research. We house a comprehensive collection of books, journals, and electronic media covering the fields of Computer Science, Information Technology, and Engineering.

With comfortable reading rooms, high-speed internet access, and dedicated zones for research, the library serves as a hub for intellectual growth and collaborative learning.

Our Commitment

Vision

To be the leading academic library in the region, recognized for its extensive digital resources and commitment to supporting research and development in computer studies.

Mission

To provide user-centered access to diverse information resources, ensuring that students and faculty have the tools they need to achieve academic excellence and contribute to the global body of knowledge.

Motivation

"We believe that books are the quietest and most constant of friends. Our goal is to foster a love for reading and learning that lasts a lifetime, empowering you to turn knowledge into wisdom."
